About IHOP

Ian's House of Pancakes was founded in 2019 by Ian Barkan. Ian came to Philadelphia in 2012 to attend art school and brought with him a love for the pancake. With our specialty being the pancake we sure know how to make a quality pancake that is hard to compare!
